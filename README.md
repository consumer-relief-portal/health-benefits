# Health Benefits Portal

Welcome to the Consumer Relief Portal's Health Benefits resource center. This portal provides comprehensive information and guidance to help consumers access healthcare coverage and benefits.

## Overview

The Health Benefits Portal is designed to assist consumers in:
- Understanding health insurance options
- Navigating marketplace enrollment
- Finding affordable coverage
- Accessing government health programs
- Understanding benefits and coverage
- Comparing insurance plans

## Features

### Core Resources
- **Insurance Options Guide** - Overview of available health plans
- **Enrollment Assistance** - Step-by-step enrollment guidance
- **Program Directory** - Government and private insurance programs
- **Benefits Comparison** - Tools to compare plans
- **FAQ Section** - Answers to common health benefits questions
- **Cost Calculator** - Estimate your costs and subsidies

### Supported Topics
- Health Insurance Marketplace (ACA)
- Medicare programs
- Medicaid eligibility and enrollment
- CHIP (Children's Health Insurance Program)
- Employer-sponsored insurance
- Short-term and catastrophic coverage
- Subsidies and tax credits
- Coverage requirements and benefits

## Important Dates

- **Open Enrollment (ACA)** - November 1 - January 15
- **Medicare Open Enrollment** - October 15 - December 7
- **Special Enrollment Periods** - Qualifying life events trigger 60-day periods
- **Medicaid** - Continuous enrollment available in most states

## Avoiding Health Insurance Scams

**Red flags to watch for:**
- Requests for upfront payment before coverage
- Guarantees of specific treatments or benefits
- Pressure to act immediately
- Requests for Social Security numbers by unsolicited callers
- Claims of exclusive or secret programs
- Misleading discount card offers
